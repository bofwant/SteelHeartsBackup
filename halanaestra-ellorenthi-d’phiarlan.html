—Backstory— Backstory: Halanaestra was the youngest child and only daughter to Gorion and Luthien Ellorenthi d’Phiarlan. She also had an older brother—Keth. She grew up being educated in the ways of music and dancing, her ethereal voice considered hauntingly beautiful by most. The girl was the epitome of grace and elegance within her family and she eventually caught the eye of the Phiarlan matriarch—Elvinor Ellorenthi d’Phiarlan. The Baron saw much promise in the young girl, wanting to mold her into the perfect spy for their house. Hal’s parents tragically passed when out on a mission for Phiarlan. Somehow their location had been leaked to a rival, so an opportunity was seized and in the end it left both Hal and Keth orphaned and alone. The matriarch of house Phiarlan used this as her chance to lure Hal deeper into her clutches and the espionage ring she led from within. Believing that Elvinor loved her like a daughter, Hal put her all into learning the ways of a spy. She did as Elvinor bid her, pleasing her greatly. That was until that fateful day that changed things forever.
Hal was approached by her brother Keth who’d refused to do as instructed by the Phiarlan matriarch. He warned her of the evil they caused and how it sickened him that she allowed herself to become a part of it. At first, Hal refused to believe her brother, thinking him jealous and full of regret for his life choices. But then he showed her proof that Elvinor played a part in their parents’ murder. Horrified, she started questioning her orders, causing Elvinor to lose patience. Elvinor became suspicious of her prodigy spy, arranging for Keth to be kidnapped—her means of leverage over Hal. To maintain control, the woman threatened to kill him, to recondition him, to torture him. The girl had no idea what Elvinor would do and when. But it was clear she needed a way to get free from Phiarlan and to find a way to rescue Keth as well. For what felt like an eternity, Hal was forced to be Elvinor’s spy... each task more dangerous than the one before it. Feeling hollow and empty, full of absolute despair, she was slowly becoming a shell of what she’d been. Unable to bear that her brother might be suffering, she eventually confronted Elvinor. The matriarch threatened to recondition her brother and have him be her replacement. She then set a bounty on the girl’s head, causing all from Phiarlan and those of her own family—Ellorenthi—to hunt her down. Terrified, she went into hiding, hoping to hone her skills and find a way to free her Keth from the evil clutches of her house’s matriarch.
Occupation: Ex-spy for house Phiarlan Why you Joined the Steel Hearts: Hal started as a favored spy of House Phiarlan, but when they kidnapped her brother and locked him away for eventual reconditioning, they’d gone too far. The Ellorenthis History: (contains spoilers) The Ellorenthis are the ruling family of House Phiarlan. The matriarch, Elvinor Ellorenthi, had a brother, along with his wife (a Phiarlan direct descendant), within her inner circle of spies. Much like the Baron, he had the trademark red hair of the Ellorenthi bloodline. When the Phiarlan Matriarch learned that her brother and his wife were going to leave her inner circle and begin working to help place a Phiarlan direct descendant upon the House Throne, she began plotting their ruin. But when it was learned that they planned to expose the truth about the Baron’s cousin having fled the house, carrying an unfavorable child, the Baron knew ruin wouldn’t be enough and so she vowed to see through the couple’s demise. It wasn’t until after things settled due to The Schism that the Baron carefully orchestrated her brother’s and his wife’s deaths, tipping off a Thuranni assassin about a conspiracy to destroy their fragile truce. This tip-off resulted in the deaths of her brother and his wife, leaving behind two orphaned children, her red-headed nephew and niece--Keth and Halanaestra Ellorenthi. Keth proved to be strong willed, lacking the malleability of his younger sister. So, the Baron left the boy alone and focused on the girl. She treated her like a daughter, embracing her warmly, listening to her life stories. Eventually, the Baron inducted the girl into her inner circle of spies. When Hal showed great promise in the area of espionage, she quickly rose through the ranks and became one of Elvinor’s most favored spies. Meanwhile, Keth kept himself distant from politics and away from the Baron’s watchful eye, and secretly began delving into history to learn what exactly had happened to their parents.
Keth never believed the stories told to him and Hal by their aunt, trying to always plant seeds of doubt within his sister’s mind since he feared her involvement in the inner circle spy ring. It wasn’t until he showed Hal a letter written by the Baron herself to another trusted spy about their parents’ plans to expose the Rookwood Controversy that she finally believed him and felt utter shame for not doing so in the first place. Her shame was doubled since she’d been assigned to hunt the family and take out the twins, not realizing they were her cousins or considered unfavorables. Upon learning this information, rather than flee, she confronted her aunt with questions. This angered the Baron and she blamed her brother for ruining everything she’d worked so hard to build. It was then that the Phiarlan Matriarch arrested Keth and dangled his fate in front of Hal, forcing her to comply for several years before she finally decided that to save Keth, she’d first need to save herself.
