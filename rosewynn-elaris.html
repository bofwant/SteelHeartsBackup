—Backstory— Backstory: Rosewynn, or Rose, was born in the Feywild. Her family was considered a bit more reclusive for the eladrin, but she didn’t know the difference. Hearing rumors that there were lands of the mortal world still wild and untouched, her family crossed over and made a life for themselves in the cold mountains. It was here that Rose came upon an elderly man who preferred the company of his animal friends and avoided people as much as possible. Their first meeting did not go over well, but Rose’s curiosity and persistence eventually won him over and he let the eladrin child become his regular visitor. One day, Rose happened upon his home, but found a polar bear instead. She feared the worst, but was happily surprised when she learned the polar bear was actually her friend. It was then that the man began teaching her magic, about life in the Arctic north. Rose was totally taken and began practicing as an Arctic Druid. She became increasingly concerned, as mortals pushed their way further and deeper into the pristine mountains. One night, a group of poachers wandered through. Rose’s mentor tried to stop them, but he was one and they were many. Her mentor was killed and brutally dismembered. She raced to her family begging for help, but they wanted nothing to do with human affairs. Disheartened and lost, she ran away from her family, keeping her distance from most out of fear and self preservation. The mountains were her love and the animals her friends. But it’s hard to learn magic without a mentor or peer. Finally facing the fact that she wasn’t meant to be totally alone, she went in search for someone willing to teach a rough-edged girl how to improve her skill, both magically and socially. —Appearance— Height: 5’0” Weight: 100lbs Picture or face claim:
