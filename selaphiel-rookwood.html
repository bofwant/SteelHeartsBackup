—Backstory— Backstory: Selaphiel and her twin brother—Barachiel—lived a lonely and isolated life with their parents. The two children were often neglected. And the times that they weren’t, they were oftentimes beaten or worse. So the two found their best friend in each other, knowing that they’d always have an ally, a friend. Someone who’d forever have their back no matter the cost. One fateful night however, Selaphiel awoke hearing blood curdling screams. Screams she recognized—the sounds of her mother. Something had happened. Something terrible. Unforgivable. The loud stomps also awoke Barachiel. Terrified, the twins followed the sounds of the screams, only to see their father’s head in one corner of the room, his body in another. Their mother was lying on the floor in a pool of blood, her words no longer coherent, but a garbled mess instead. They watched their mother die, a great beast watching on in glee.
The creature eyed the two, grabbing Selaphiel first. The young elf girl thrashed and fought, desperate to get free from the beast’s clutches. But she wasn’t strong enough. So Barachiel interceded, freeing his sister and being captured in return. The young elf girl charged after the creature, though he merely batted her away, slicing up her face as a reminder. Lying on the floor, unable to move, she watched the mammoth beast stalk off with her brother. Her other half. His shouts still sounded in her ears—awake, dreaming, she’d always hear that oni’s voice in her mind and whatever force of nature that caused him to destroy what little happiness she had. Determined to one day reunite with her brother, despite his chances for survival being next to nothing, she went into hiding, honing what little skills she had, knowing she bore a dark mark. A cursed mark. When it became clear that she’d never see her twin again, she became reckless, testing her fate, pushing her limits. After all, she had nothing more to live for. Nothing more to love. And no one could love someone so broken and haunted.

The Rookwoods History: (contains spoilers) Selaphiel and Barachiel’s mother was a noble of house Phiarlan, specifically of the Ellorenthi family and therefore first cousin to the Phiarlan Matriarch. Their father was of an unfavorable family, considered bad elven blood for further breeding those marked by the shadow. Their parents, by some stroke of fate, happened to meet and connect in a way that compelled both to meet again in secret and succumb to carnal pleasures. The result of this secret meeting was that their mother became pregnant with an unfavorable’s child. She knew she could not remain with her noble family, especially since she was Ellorenthi, of the ruling family of the shadow marked. As a result, she fled with her lover and the two secretly married and remained hidden until she surprisingly gave birth to twins. Immediately, the mother saw this as a bad omen, but their father did not. Over the years, while the twins grew, the parents began to grow distant from one another. The mother was missing her luxurious life at court and growing both jealous and disgusted at watching her husband fall in love with their daughter. As a result, she grew very bitter towards Selaphiel, exhibiting both psychological and verbal abuse towards the girl. Meanwhile, the father was sexually abusive towards Selaphiel, exploiting the girl’s beauty not just for his own pleasure, but for his friends as well. These behaviors often caused Barachiel to intervene and protect his sister. Typically, this results in her brother getting beaten or worse.
One of the ways, Selaphiel’s mother attempted to cope was by dabbling in the darker arts. The woman’s hope was to bring herself and her husband closer together and try to find a way for them to return to life with the Phiarlan family. Though Selaphiel did not know it, she was their pawn during all of this, their means of luring otherworldly beings to assist them in their task. Terrified of what her parents were doing, she kept this whole ordeal secret from her brother, though he always suspected something more was going on. Later on, Selaphiel has blocked out most of her memory of what happened, but from time to time she will have nightmares or experience triggering events that remind her of that horrific connection to the demonic world. The twins did their best to cope with their horrible life until one night everything changed forever. An oni attacked their home, killing both parents and attempting to kidnap Selaphiel. Barachiel intervened and ended up kidnapped instead. Selaphiel tried to save her brother, but ended up being too weak, injured terribly and left with a horrible scar across her face. She then set out to rescue him, in hopes to reunite with him once more.
