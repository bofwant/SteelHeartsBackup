—Backstory— Backstory: Irithriel grew up in the Feywild, the daughter of elven nobility. She lived a carefree life, given free rein to roam anywhere she chose. The capricious girl found comfort in music, listening to all the skilled musicians play and entertain as often as she could. She became utterly enamored and, while her family spent most of its time on matters of safety and security, she would run wild and play, her days filled with music and dance and even some magic. She became known as the ‘Wild Princess’ among the people and as a ‘nuisance’ to those at court. Though she was beloved by her family, they became frustrated with her lackadaisical ways, always putting things off for tomorrow or another day entirely. One day, they were traveling to a neighboring kingdom when she accidentally slipped and fell and magically transported through the crossing—the portal between the Feywild and the mortal world. Lost and unable to get home, she decides to make it on her own, proving she’s got more worth than the stuffy court gave her credit for. Occupation: None yet, merely wandering.

The Ravenwoods History: (contains spoilers) The Ravenwoods married into the Phiarlan family. The most notable Ravenwood family included the daughter of the founding Phiarlan Patriarch and a noble high elf considered worthy to breed with those marked by the shadow. Secret plots brewed to one day set this particular pair upon the Phiarlan Throne, albeit secretly, so as not to anger the currently ruling Baron Elvinor Ellorenthi. The pair had two children--Vaegon and Irithriel. Vaegon had all the features and manner of his Phiarlan family (white/blonde hair, pale skin, gray/blue eyes), and while Irithriel also bore the features of her noble Phiarlan family, she was considered more of a wildling, at least by those attending court. Vae was groomed to one day lead the Phiarlan family, several hopeful to see him take back the power and sit on the throne. Oblivious to politics, Irithriel roamed wild, learning magic, dance, music, and art. Due to Irithriel’s growing disposition as one considered a nuisance and laughing stock of the Phiarlan name, her parents quickly made arrangements to marry her off to someone from the Shol family--Evindal. Evindal was also best friends with Irithriel’s older brother, Vae, the two men always bullying her and tormenting her at every turn. Evindal had originally accepted the betrothal to the one nicknamed ‘the wild princess’ since his own family supported Vae taking the throne, thinking such a match would secure them power. But eventually Evindal could no longer bear the whispers, so he renounced the engagement, angering the girl’s entire family including her brother. For, it would fall on Vae to take care of her should she not find a suitable husband and continue breeding their family bloodline and the worry was that it would be hard for him to marry and bear heirs with such a sister underfoot.
It did not take long for Vae to come up with a plan to rid the family of Irithriel and her reputation. When called to gather by the High Elves of the Feywild (as a war between the Seelie and Unseelie Courts was brewing, all manner of elves and sylvans caught in the crossfire), he seized the opportunity to abandon his sister mid journey. Being naive and very unaware of things, the girl ended up lost and accidentally crossing into the Material Plane, finding herself in the Kingdom of Eire.
